Blankslate

Blankslates are for when there is a lack of content within a page or section. Use them as placeholders to tell users why something isnâ€™t there. Be sure to provide an action to add content as well.

Basic example

Wrap some content in the outer .blankslate wrapper to give it the blankslate appearance.

This is a blank slate

Use it to provide information when no dynamic content exists.

<div class="blankslate">
<h3>This is a blank slate</h3>
<p>Use it to provide information when no dynamic content exists.</p>
</div>

With Octicons

When it helps the message, include (relevant) icons in your blank slate. Add .blankslate-icon to any .mega-octicons as the first elements in the blankslate, like so.

Variations
